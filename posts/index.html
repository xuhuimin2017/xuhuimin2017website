Chapter II: 研究项目

Beijing mobility The social classes of people have important impact on our behaviors in both the virtual world and physical space. Nowadays, digital media provides a new lens for researchers to investigate people’s allocation of attention in the virtual world and their mobility in the physical space. Using a smartphone dataset of 4125 people, we tried to study how social class influences our moving behaviors in both online and offline space.
